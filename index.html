about me

born in mexico, my journey began with a profound fascination for understanding human culture, leading me to study social anthropology. i enjoy writing short stories, gaming, diving into history and learning new languages. recently i discovered a passion for web development, mastering html, css, and javascript. along the way, i've been captivated by the endless creative possibilities of AI and technology.

more about me

i studied Social Anthropology at the Autonomous University of Yucatan, where i conducted research on gender identity and wrote about non-binary individuals. i also hold a diploma in creative writing from the Centro Estatal de Bellas Artes in Merida, Yucatan.

my professional experience includes working in copywriting for marketing projects and serving as a bilingual interpreter for medical services temporarily. after visiting Germany and developing an interest in its culture, i am now learning the language.
